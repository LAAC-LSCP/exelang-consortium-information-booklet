# ExELang Consortium Information Booklet

Welcome to the ExELang Consortium Information Booklet. This document describes how the ExELang Consortium works: who can do what with which data, under what conditions, and who is responsible for what. It is primarily intended for custodians and corpus collectors, but can also be useful for institutional review boards, legal and compliance departments, and scientific archives.
